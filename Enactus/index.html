About Enactus IIT Roorkee

Enactus IIT Roorkee is a student-run organization committed to creating a positive impact through entrepreneurial action.

Our vision is to transform lives and empower communities by addressing social, environmental, and economic challenges.

We aim to achieve our mission by implementing sustainable projects that generate tangible and measurable outcomes.

Our Roorkee

  • Highlighting Roorkee 's cultural heritage for tourism enhancement.

Ark

The Essence Of Nature

  • Using floral waste from temples to produce some useful organic products by recycling the waste.
  • Promoting Organic & Eco- friendly products, and saving the environment from harmful chemicals.
  • We train rural women from economically weaker section for the production hence empowering women.
  • We established a circular economy in the floral industry by utilizing the potential of Floral waste.

Khoj

The Tourism Intiative

  • Project KHOJ is a tourism-related initiative in which we
  • Research villages or places with the potential to become widely known as tourist attractions.
  • Our goal is to make sure that tourists have new places to travel to.
  • Make sure that the villagers benefit from these arrangements.

Anushruti

Deaf and Hard of hearing community (DHH)

  • An AI model that converts ISL to text in real time.
  • The model will take real time video as an input and will output the message.
  • It begins with collecting a diverse ISL dataset, annotating it with corresponding text translations, and preprocessing for consistency.
  • We train the model on this dataset and then test the model with real time predictions.
